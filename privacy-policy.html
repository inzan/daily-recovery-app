Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

Welcome to Daily Recovery App ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Daily Recovery App for iOS.

Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including prayer tracking frequency, app usage times, and feature usage.
  • User Content: Any notes, reflections, or custom prayers you add to the app.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our app functionality
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send notifications and reminders you have requested
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our app

Data Storage and Security

All of your personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your prayer data, reflections, or personal notes on our servers. This means your spiritual journey remains private and secure on your personal device.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Analytics: Used to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app experience.

Each of these services collects and processes data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data stored in the app
  • Disable analytics collection through your device settings
  • Request information about how your data is processed

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
